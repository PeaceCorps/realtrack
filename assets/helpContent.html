Getting Started with RealTrack

  1. Create a project and fill in details relevant to all activities within that project. (Example- Girls Club)

  2. Create an activity within a project and fill in details relevant to all instances of that activity (Example- Study Sessions)

  3. Set a reminder to record participation/notes. (Example- Since the study sessions are recurring on every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon you would set a reminder on those evenings to record participation)

  4. Fill in notes from a project activity's participation each time. (Example- for each study session you would enter the number of participants and any other relative details.)

  5. You can create additional projects or activities as needed by clicking on the + icon on the upper right or by clicking on "Add a new Activity..."

  6. Going forward, view all your projects by clicking on "My Projects" and record participation/notes for a new instance of an activity by clicking on the people icon.

  7. View an organized list of all your data by clicking on "My Data" from the home screen.

  8. Export your data into a spreadsheet and email to yourself to use when filling the VRF by clicking on My Data and then the email icon on the top right.
