Getting Started with RealTrack

  1. Create a project and fill in details relevant to all activities within that project. (Example- Youth Development)

  2. Create an activity within a project and fill in details relevant to all instances of that activity (Example- English Classes)

  3. Set a reminder to record participation/notes. (Example- Since the study sessions are recurring on every Monday and Thursday afternoon you would set a reminder on those evenings to record participation)

  4. Create a single event within an activity and add some notes or record participation (Example – each time you taught an English Class you would enter the number of participants and any other details.)

  5. If you like, you can bring your tablet to events and let participants sign-in electronically. Click on date and time and they’ll automatically put the current time. Don’t worry, if you forget to record an event, you can always go back later. Then, have your participants sign-in on the tablet.

  6. You can create additional projects or activities as needed by clicking on the + icon on the upper right or by clicking on "Add a new Activity..."

  7. Going forward, view all your projects by clicking on "My Projects" and record participation/notes for a new instance of an activity by clicking on the people icon.

  8. View an organized list and graph of all your data by clicking on "My Data" from the home screen.

  9. Export your data into a spreadsheet and email to yourself to use when filling the VRF by clicking on My Data and then the email icon on the top right.
