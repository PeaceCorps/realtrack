RealTrack Glossary

ParticipantIndividual, service provider, organization or community who participates in a Volunteer's primary or secondary activities.
IndividualsPeople that Volunteers assist or reach directly, for example, community members, students, farmers, or small business owners.
Service ProvidersPeople who provided direct training, education, or services to other during the activity are people (e.g. teachers, health care educators, agricultural extension agents, community leaders, peer educators)
OrganizationsSchools, governmental or non-governmental organizations, businesses, community-based organizations, health clinics, informal community groups, associations, cooperatives, and others.
Reporting CohortUnique identifier for the group that participated in an activity. (e.g. Memorial Elementary 3rd Grade)
CommunitiesCommunities are groups of people living in the same geographic area, usually a village or neighborhood.
